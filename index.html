Employ Ukrainians

Facebook Page - LinkedIn Page - Instagram Profile

Job Seekers

This is an initiative to help all Ukrainians, citizens or not, finding employment and jobs. Please share your CV or resume via this Job Seeker's Form.

Employers

This is a humble call to the employers, companies, corporates, govts and states to come forward in this cause. Share your jobs (via this Employer's Form) you wish to hire and employ Ukrainians for.

Volunteers

Also calling for volunteers like recruiters, immigration specialists, Ukrainian speakers etc. to help this initiative workable. Fill-up this Volunteer's Form to begin contributing.

Note: Our resources are growing now, additional to Volunteers and LinkedIn Premium, we are also in talks with local Labor Offices (and organizations) of the countries welcoming Ukrainians. There is no charge or fee for the employers if they Employ Ukrainians from this initiative.

Want to Contribute?

You may say "what can I do?", We say, "you do what you can do!". Calling for Recruiters, Head Hunters, Ukrainian Speakers, CV/Resume Specialists, Immigration Specialists and so on...

Ukrainian Speakers

Are you an Ukrainian Speaker and wish to help with documentation translation, communicating in Ukrainian language, and help on this front then please contact us.

Employers

We invite you to share jobs with us. This is free i.e. there is no charge or fee if you hire and employ Ukrainians from this initiative. Please fill up this Google Form to Share Job Description if you have one or few jobs to share, or contact us.

Recruiters & Head Hunters

If you want to contribute as a recruiter to help manage job posts, job applications, qualify candidates, line up interviews then please contact us.

Immigration Specialists

If labor law of the country is your speciality, you know the paperwork for helping Ukrainians to get required visa or approvals, and wish to help onboard the selected candidates then please contact us.

Social Media & Content Experts

If you wish to contribute managing and creating Content, and also manage social media accounts to spread the word and boost this initiative then please contact us.

CV & Resume Experts

Are you an expert to redesign or recreate accurate CVs for the candidates, do you wish to contribute as a person who helps candidate prepare better with interviews then please contact us.
